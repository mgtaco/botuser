This server is dedicated to Aftermath.
Aftermath is an Apocalypse Survival Game on Roblox in which you gain good loot and weapons, fight the hazardous Zombies, and battle with other players. It's exciting, with beautiful graphics, endless play-ability, and full of adventure, whether you are running from Zombies, shooting down a squad of four players, hoping for the best guns in the next locker, exploring the scenery, or searching for the lost mysteries hidden under the cover of an Apocalypse.
