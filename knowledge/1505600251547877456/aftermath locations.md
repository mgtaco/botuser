# Roblox Aftermath Locations

## Airfield
Tier 3 | NW map | Large ex-civilian airport turned military base.
Buildings: 2 terminals, ATC tower, 8 barracks, 6 hangars, 3 offices, 3 military warehouses, N/W checkpoints, quarantine zone.
Loot: Best consistent military weapons, ammo, armour, attachments. Hangars/warehouses are top spots.
Pros: Huge loot quantity and quality, multiple buildings. Cons: Highest PvP danger on map, large area to clear. Note: Russian Raid event triggered by Mysterious CD-rom.

## Almosta Town
Tier 2 | North map | Mid-sized town west of Quarry.
Buildings: Police department, church, construction site, apartments.
Loot: Civilian weapons, food, meds, basic clothing. Police station best for pistols/ammo.
Pros: Decent variety, less contested, good police station. Cons: No military loot, far north from southern routes.

## Quarry
Tier 2 | NE map | Large quarry pit with merged military base (formerly Quarry M.B., merged Nov 2024).
Buildings: Admin building, blue silo, 4 hazmat tents (need hazmat suit), giant excavator, military base.
Loot: Mid-tier in admin/silo, higher-tier in hazmat tents, some military gear from base section.
Pros: Large area, rewarding hazmat tents, military section. Cons: Hazmat tents dangerous without suit, open terrain = sniper exposure.

## Bomb Shelter
Tier 2 (hidden high-tier) | NE map | Small underground bunker. Requires Black Floppy Disk + 0.5kg Biofuel to enter.
Buildings: Computer room, top/middle/bottom floors.
Loot: Bottom Floor Table: 45% MRAD, 40% AWM, other rare .50 BMG/.308 rifles. Other floors: general military loot.
Pros: Best weapon rolls in game, rarely contested, safe inside. Cons: Requires Black Floppy Disk and Biofuel every visit.

## Chestnut Oaks
Tier 1 | NE map | Residential suburb south of Bomb Shelter.
Buildings: Houses, small shops, garages.
Loot: Food, clothing, melee weapons, occasional low-tier firearms, crafting materials.
Pros: Very low PvP, good early-game, crafting materials. Cons: Extremely high zombie density, poor loot late-game.

## Industrial
Tier 1-2 | Central map | Large industrial complex north of Paradise.
Buildings: Mechanic bays, 2 office buildings, 6 vehicle garages.
Loot: Best location for duct tape, wrenches, glass. Some civilian weapons and basic ammo.
Pros: Best crafting material farm, low PvP. Cons: Very poor weapon loot, skip if not crafting.

## Flat Peak
Tier 2 | Central map | Small town east of Radio Tower.
Buildings: Barn (T13 Crossbow spawn), basement house (supply/ammo crates), residential buildings.
Loot: T13 Crossbow (barn), ammo and supply crates (basement), standard civilian loot.
Pros: Unique crossbow spawn, basement crates, low traffic. Cons: Small, no military loot.

## Redfield
Tier 2 | West map | Large city north of Airfield. Reworked May 2025.
Buildings: Hospital, military base, residential/commercial blocks.
Loot: Medical supplies (hospital excellent for meds), military gear from base, civilian weapons/food.
Pros: Best hospital/meds in game, military base, large city. Cons: Moderate-high PvP, far west.

## Radio Tower
Tier 2 (hidden high-tier) | Central map | Communications hub with attached military base.
Buildings: Radio station, 2 barracks, 3 warehouses, 1 watchtower, the tower itself.
Loot: Military weapons/ammo from base. Blue Floppy Disk + Biofuel at tower = special airdrop (high-tier).
Pros: Airdrop mechanic, solid military base, central location. Cons: Airdrop requires resources and attracts players.

## Crescent Grove
Tier 1 | West map | Small town south of Redfield.
Buildings: Gas station, repair shop, houses, military outpost (between Crescent Grove and Redfield).
Loot: Food, fuel, tools, repair/crafting materials. Light military gear from outpost.
Pros: Very low PvP, good for fuel and materials. Cons: Low-tier loot, small, pitstop only.

## Survivor Camp
Tier 1-2 | SW map | Small fortified settlement. Requires Biofuel in generator to open gate.
Buildings: Gated entrance, shipping container bridge, broken military truck, survivor structures.
Loot: Mix of civilian and light military supplies, food, meds. Truck may have weapon loot.
Pros: Gated entry reduces random encounters, quiet, low traffic. Cons: Requires Biofuel, modest loot for the effort.

## Prison
Tier 2 | SW map | Large prison complex near New Haven and Redfield.
Buildings: Warden's office, big/small cell blocks, armory, trailer camp, laundry buildings, secret tunnel.
Loot: Military weapons and armour from armory, mid-tier loot in cell blocks, better items in warden's office.
Pros: Armory is reliable military weapon source, secret tunnel for tactics, many loot rooms. Cons: High PvP, enclosed = dangerous firefights, high zombie density in corridors.

## New Haven
Tier 2 | SW map | Large city SE of Prison. Added May 2025, formerly called Rosewood.
Buildings: Town Hall (military base inside), quarantine zone, residential/commercial districts.
Loot: Military gear from Town Hall base, quarantine zone (high risk/reward), standard city loot.
Pros: Military base in Town Hall, large city, quarantine zone option. Cons: Newer location = frequently visited, quarantine zone needs gear.

## Pine Hill
Tier 1 | SW-Central map | Small decaying town SW of Camp Ground. Revamped May 2025.
Buildings: Waterfall basin, car repair shop, diner, gas station.
Loot: Food, fuel, basic civilian items.
Pros: Very low PvP, good early-game food/fuel. Cons: Low-tier only, no military loot, small.

## Saw Mill
Tier 1-2 | South map | Industrial sawmill between Nuclear Powerplant and Prison.
Buildings: Blue silo building, fortified docking bay, factory.
Loot: Industrial materials, tools, some mid-tier weapons/ammo, crafting supplies.
Pros: Decent spread across 3 buildings, strategic position. Cons: Not a top destination, surrounded by high-danger zones.

## Nuclear Powerplant
Tier 3 | SE map | High-danger military site west of Saw Mill. Interior has toxic gas.
Buildings: Reactor building (toxic inside), 3 warehouses, 2 nuclear silos.
Loot: High-tier military weapons and armour, rare items in reactor, quality warehouse loot.
Pros: Top-tier loot rivalling Airfield, less visited due to difficulty. Cons: Hazmat suit required inside, Chinese Soldier Zombies (stronger than normal), high PvP risk.
Note: Only location where Chinese Soldier Zombies spawn.

## Paradise
Tier 2 | East-Central map | Largest civilian city. Semi-deserted and sprawling.
Buildings: Police station, fire station, hospital, military base, hunting store.
Loot: Police station (pistols, ammo), hospital (meds), military base (weapons/gear), hunting store (rifles, crossbows).
Pros: Best loot variety of any city, something for every playstyle. Cons: Very large and time-consuming, moderate-high PvP.

## Camp Ground
Tier 1 (niche) | South-Central map | Woodland area with 8 hunting cabins north of Pine Hill.
Buildings: 8 hunting cabins.
Loot: Best location for MP-133 shotgun and Mosin Nagant. Hunting supplies, food, basic gear.
Pros: Best MP-133 and Mosin Nagant spawns, very low PvP. Cons: Limited loot otherwise, cabins spread out, no military loot.

## Bunker
Tier 4 (only Tier 4 location) | Far SW map | Most exclusive location. Requires Red Floppy Disk to access.
Buildings: NPC-guarded interior rooms, high-tier loot rooms.
NPCs: 15 armed Chinese NPCs with SKS, Saiga-12, PKM, or AKM. They actively shoot and patrol.
Loot: Best loot in the entire game. Top-tier weapons, armour, rare/exclusive Tier 4 items.
Pros: Highest loot quality, exclusive Tier 4 drops, key-gated limits traffic. Cons: Red Floppy Disk required (very rare), 15 active NPC enemies, far SW, extremely high stakes.
