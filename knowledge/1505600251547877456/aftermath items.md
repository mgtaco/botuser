# Roblox Aftermath Key Items

## Black Floppy Disk (Mysterious Floppy Disc 1)
Use: Opens the Bomb Shelter vault.
How to get: Kill a Tactical Zombie. Spawns anywhere military zombies spawn at 1/1000 chance. Best farmed at Airfield. 100% drop rate.
Rarity: Very rare (1/1000 military zombie spawn).
Image: https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/quarantine-aftermath/images/f/f8/Black_Floppy.png/revision/latest?cb=20240718174202

## Blue Floppy Disk (Mysterious Floppy Disc 2)
Use: Triggers airdrop at Radio Tower. Requires ~2kg Biofuel in generator at tower base, then insert disk into laptop at top.
How to get: 1/1000 chance drop from ANY zombie (civilian or military).
Warning: Airdrop lands within 100m — attracts nearby players.
Rarity: Most common of the three floppies.
Image: https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/quarantine-aftermath/images/0/0b/Blue_Floppy.png/revision/latest?cb=20240718201434

## Red Floppy Disk (Mysterious Floppy Disc 3)
Use: Unlocks the Bunker.
How to get: Kill a Chinese Zombie inside Nuclear Powerplant. 2.5% chance to spawn in place of a military zombie. 100% drop rate. 10 spawn points mainly near military tents and Reactor Building exits. Full hazmat suit required to enter.
Rarity: Rare (hazmat zone + 2.5% spawn).
Image: https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/quarantine-aftermath/images/3/3d/Red_Floppy.png/revision/latest?cb=20240718175848

## Mysterious CD-Rom
Use: Triggers the Chinook Helicopter (Russian Raid) event at Airfield.
How to get: Spawns on the Bomb Shelter main loot table (same table as AWM, MK-14 EBR). Requires Black Floppy to access Bomb Shelter first.
How to use: Airfield ATC Tower — place 0.3kg+ Biofuel in generator at base, climb to top, insert CD into computer.
Event: Chinook flies in over Almosta Town. Three bombers drop six gas bombs gassing most of Airfield. Four Russian Hazmat Soldier NPCs exit the helicopter. Helicopter despawns after 30 minutes (blows up at 20 min, wreck despawns at 10 min). Cannot trigger another raid during this window.
Chinook Loot — two sources:
  Russian Soldier Bodies (kill the 4 NPCs): Gas Mask, Hazmat Shirt, Hazmat Pants, SKS, AKM, Makarov, PKM, SVD, AS-VAL, Saiga-12, ammo (7.62 Soviet, 12 Gauge, 7.62x54mmR, 9MM, 39x9MM).
  Helicopter Weapon Crate (inside Chinook): Military clothing (BDU, Woodland, Desert variants), Military Vest, Operator/Military Helmet, Rucksack, Black Boots, ammo. Uncommon: M249, FAMAS, G36K, SPAS-12, M4A1. Also M79. Guaranteed Red Floppy Disk inside.
Rarity: Extremely rare. One of the best loot events in the game.
